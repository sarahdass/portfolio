    • Feed the Cats!

    Feed the Cats! is a simple game where players collect fish and give it to the correct cat. The cats are picky and are only happy when given the same color fish that they are thinking of. If the player gives the cat the wrong fish they will lose points!

    Escape the Farm!!

    In Escape the Farm!!, the player is a chicken who is, well, trying to escape the farm. Players must move boxes around and jump to figure out how to escape the areas.

    Raindrops

    Raindrops is similar to Escape the Farm, but it adds more complexity to the puzzles by requiring the player to find keys.
